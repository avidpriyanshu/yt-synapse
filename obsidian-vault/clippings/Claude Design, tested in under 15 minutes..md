Anthropic just launched Claude Design — their new AI design tool that turns prompts into websites, prototypes, and slide decks. It dropped yesterday (April 17, 2026), and I spent the night testing it on a real product: Mave, a brain wearable that uses tDCS technology.  
  
I ran three rounds with escalating context — one-line prompt, then positioning, then a full designer brief — to see how much the input actually matters. What I found changed what I thought this video was going to be about.  
  
If you're a designer, a founder thinking about hiring design help, or someone trying to figure out what AI tools actually mean for creative work right now — this is an honest, first-day take from a working product designer.  
  
Claude Design is available now in research preview for Claude Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise subscribers. Powered by Claude Opus 4.7.
